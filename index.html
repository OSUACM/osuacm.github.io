About Us

var ACMClub = new ACM.studentChapter();

Purpose

OSU ACM Club is aimed at supporting students to participate in programming contests, and other events including hackathons, research, and open-source.

Membership

We are open membership. You DON'T need to apply to become a member. Our members are eligible to participate in various ACM events and receive reduced rates on fees.

Events

We hold weekly meetings in Fall and Spring semesters, and special events like Winter Coding Camp. The qualifier round of yearly ACM-ICPC is also held by us.

About ACM

Association of Computing Machinery (ACM) is one of the largest worldwide associations of computing professionals. We are the student chapter of ACM in OSU
